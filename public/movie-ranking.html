The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception (2010)

A mind-bending sci-fi thriller about a thief who infiltrates dreams to plant an idea,
blurring the lines between reality and illusion; you’ll love this movie for its intelligent
plot twists and stunning visuals that keep you thinking long after it ends.

The Pursuit of Happyness (2006)

An inspiring true story of a struggling father who overcomes adversity to build a better life for his son;
it’s a movie that resonates deeply with its powerful message of resilience and hope.

The Dark Knight (2008)

A gripping superhero epic where Batman faces his greatest challenge in the form of the chaotic and menacing Joker;
you’ll love it for its intense action and Heath Ledger's unforgettable performance.
